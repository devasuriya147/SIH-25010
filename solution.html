Smart Crop Advisory System — Solution Overview

Proposed Solution

A mobile-first, multilingual advisory platform that provides real-time, location-specific recommendations for small and marginal farmers. Key features include:

  • AI-based pest and disease detection using image uploads.
  • Soil health and fertilizer guidance tailored to local conditions.
  • Weather-based alerts and predictive insights for crop management.
  • Market price tracking to aid crop selling decisions.
  • Voice support for low-literate users.
  • Feedback collection to improve recommendations over time.

Innovation & Uniqueness

  • Hybrid on-device and cloud AI for offline and online usage.
  • Adaptive fertilizer plans based on soil tests and crop stage.
  • Federated learning to improve recommendations while preserving privacy.

Technical Approach

  • Frontend: React Native or Progressive Web App for mobile devices.
  • Backend: Node.js or Python FastAPI, Postgres for structured data, object store for images.
  • Machine Learning: TensorFlow or PyTorch for pest detection; SciKit/XGBoost for predictive advisory models.
  • Weather & Mapping: OpenStreetMap, IMD or other weather APIs.
  • Voice & Multilingual Support: Local TTS/ASR or cloud services, i18n frameworks.

Methodology

Farmer registers → app collects location & crop info → soil & historical data linked → real-time pest detection & weather alerts → personalized advisory + market prices → feedback loop for improvement.

Impact & Benefits

  • Higher yields and reduced input costs.
  • Reduced environmental damage from chemical overuse.
  • Data-driven insights for government and NGOs.

Expected Outcomes

Multilingual Soil Health Weather Alerts Pest Detection Voice Support Market Tracking
